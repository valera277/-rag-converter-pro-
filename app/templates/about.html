About RAG Converter Pro

RAG Converter Pro is a lightweight web app that prepares documents for retrieval‑augmented generation. It converts PDF and TXT files into clean, structured datasets that are easy to index and search.

What it does

  • Extracts text from PDF and TXT files
  • Cleans headers, page numbers, and extra whitespace
  • Splits content into consistent chunks for RAG pipelines
  • Provides a ready‑to‑use dataset file

Who it is for

Teams building AI assistants, support search, or internal knowledge bases can use RAG Converter Pro to speed up data preparation and keep datasets consistent.
